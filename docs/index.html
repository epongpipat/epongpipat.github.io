  About Me


I am primarly interested in understanding the relationship between brain networks and cognition and how this relationship can change as we age and for different groups of individuals (e.g., disease-disorder states and genetic predispositions). I’m also interested in statistics, research methods, intuitive data visualization, open source, and open science.
